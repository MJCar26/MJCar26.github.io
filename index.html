About

Facts

In April 2008, Rocky the Grizzly Bear, who wrestles Jackie Moon, killed Stephan Miller, one of his trainers and a cousin of Will Ferrell's stunt double, by biting him on the neck.

In the film, Jackie Moon says that he had never vomited before, not even as a baby. In reality, Will Ferrell suffered from pyloric stenosis, a condition that causes frequent vomiting, as a baby. A scar on his upper right abdomen, from the surgery he had to treat this condition, appears in this and many other films.

During the Tropics game with the San Antonio Spurs, a banner can be seen on the sideline that reads "WOAI". These are the call letters for an actual San Antonio radio sation (AM 1200) that continues to carry San Antonio Spurs games to this day.
